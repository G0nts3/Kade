The Method

Everything begins on paper he cuts himself.

No commission is drawn digitally until the final permit set. Every building starts as a graphite section, redrawn by hand — by Kade alone — an average of forty-one times before a single wall is costed.

Clients are not shown renders. They are shown one physical model, lit by a single bulb, in a room he chooses.

STAGE 01The site is visited only at the hour the client least expects.
STAGE 02A single section is drawn before a single plan exists.
STAGE 03The model is built at 1:50, in raw materials, never painted.
STAGE 04Construction is supervised in person, every week, without exception.
Access

He accepts one commission a year.

There is no proposal process. Write to the studio. Describe the site, not the building you imagine. He will decide within one month whether the site deserves him.
